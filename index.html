About us

The GDL Discord is a Discord community that was created on the 23rd of November 2016 originally as a graphics server. The original meaning of GDL meaning Graphics Designers Lounge it was mainly a server for graphics. Although as the server grew larger we seen it to be potentially bigger and changed it as an all round community with gaming, music, developers, YouTubers and of course keeping the main source of graphics. We decided to do this to reach out to more members and offer the community a wider range of options. The server has almost reached 300 members which is all thanks to you guys! On the behalf of myself I thank the Owners for keeping the place running and our wonderful staff team for keeping the chats clean and calm and helping all of our wonderful members! Once again on my behalf enjoy your time in the server!
